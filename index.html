A little about me

I am a developer that started out in graphic design. Over time my graphic design gravitated towards designing and building websites with Wordpress. I became familiar with HTML5 and CSS. I knew that I wanted to do more though. In August 2022 I started at DevMountain in order to learn front-end development. Now I can proudly call myself a developer.

I have an insatiable need to learn and love to solve problems. I will always continue to educate myself and grow. When I am not working on a project you will probably find me watching a new documentary, hanging out with my family, or finding lost dogs and returning them to their families.
